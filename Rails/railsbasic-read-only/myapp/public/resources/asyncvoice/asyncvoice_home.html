What is Async Voice?

Async Voice is a service that lets you talk to your friends using short (< 14s) voice messages. It's push-to-talk, made for the web!

What do I need?

  • A microphone connected to your computer
  • Flash 10.1 or newer.
  • A modern browser (show).
    Note Internet Explorer is currently not supported.
  • An Async Voice account
